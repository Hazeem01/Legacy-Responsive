Eligibility

  1. You must be at least 18 and not more than 60 years of age
  2. You must be a resident of this community
  3. You should be available for volunteer services for at least 10 hours a week
  4. You should be a citizen of an African country, or have spent at least 3 years in any ECOWAS state or 5 years in any African country aside ECOWAS
  5. You agree that your services would be free of charge, you are not expecting any monetary reward or compensation (NB: We do provide awards and Certificate for qualified participants)
  6. You agree to our Terms and Conditions
